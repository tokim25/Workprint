# Report Types

## Validate
Inventory evidence, identify missing sources, and assess likely confidence.

## Timeline
Prioritize chronology, dependencies, conflicts, gaps, and evidence locators.

## Attribution
Explain task categories, contributors, human review, AI material used or discarded, confidence, and alternatives.

## Executive
Summarize scope, strongest findings, important unknowns, confidence, and limitations.

## Portfolio
Emphasize the problem, responsibilities, decisions, AI use, validation, outcomes, and evidence-supported language.

## Detailed
Include complete inventory, event IDs, finding IDs, reasoning, alternatives, limitations, and reproducibility notes.
