A clearer record of human and AI collaboration

See what you did, what AI did, and how the work came together.

Workprint reviews the places where a project happened and turns the available evidence into a careful, understandable story.

Workprint does not assign contribution percentages or claim the evidence can determine authorship.

What Workprint cannot determine

The available evidence cannot prove authorship, effort, ownership, intent, or the complete history of every decision. This discovery is an interpretation of visible patterns, not a definitive attribution.
