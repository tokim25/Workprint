# Evidence Collection Guide

Collect the minimum evidence needed to answer the question.

## Human and AI contribution

Strong sources: original AI chats, version history, Git diffs, review records, and the final artifact.

## Active time

Strong sources: direct time trackers, IDE activity with active/idle detection, and project calendars. Git alone is not enough for precise time reporting.

## Decisions

Strong sources: decision logs, meeting transcripts, approval comments, issue discussions, and versioned design documents.

## Privacy checklist

Remove unrelated personal information, redact secrets and customer data, confirm authorization to share team communications, minimize excerpts, and identify evidence that cannot leave its source system.
