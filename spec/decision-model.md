# Decision Model

Major product decisions should be represented explicitly.

## Structure

- Decision ID
- Question
- Decision
- Rationale
- Supporting evidence
- Confidence
- Alternatives considered
- Date (if known)

## Example

Decision ID: D-0001

Question:
Should Workprint estimate human vs. AI contribution percentages?

Decision:
No.

Rationale:
Percentages imply precision that the evidence generally cannot support.

Confidence:
High

Supporting evidence:
Conversation history from project inception.
