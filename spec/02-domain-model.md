# Domain Model

- **Project:** the bounded body of work under investigation.
- **Actor:** a human, AI system, automated service, or unknown contributor.
- **Artifact:** a file, document, design, message, commit, build, or AI response.
- **Evidence:** a source record made available for analysis.
- **Observation:** a statement directly supported by evidence.
- **Event:** an observation anchored to a time or interval.
- **Activity:** the type of work represented by an event.
- **Finding:** an evidence-backed interpretation.
- **Estimate:** a bounded quantitative inference with assumptions.
- **Conclusion:** a synthesis of findings.
- **Report:** a presentation of evidence, findings, estimates, unknowns, and limitations.
