# Investigation Protocol

1. Define question and scope.
2. Inventory evidence.
3. Assess quality and completeness.
4. Normalize evidence into events.
5. Reconstruct chronology.
6. Classify activities.
7. Attribute activities.
8. Calculate measured metrics and bounded estimates.
9. Test alternative explanations.
10. Produce an audience-specific report.

Each material finding should have a unique ID, evidence IDs, reasoning, confidence, and relevant alternatives.
