# Reporting Standard

Every report includes scope, evidence inventory, material findings, finding-level confidence, unknowns, limitations, and evidence references.

Use “The evidence shows...” for direct observations, “The available evidence supports...” for findings, “Workprint estimates...” for estimates, and “Unable to determine...” for unknowns.

Avoid unsupported certainty, total human-versus-AI percentages, minimizing language, or claims of legal ownership.
