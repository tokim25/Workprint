# Workprint Principles

1. Evidence before conclusions.
2. Observation and inference are different.
3. Measured, estimated, and unknown remain distinct.
4. Activity attribution is not legal ownership.
5. A generated artifact is not necessarily an adopted artifact.
6. Unknown is a legitimate finding.
7. Every material conclusion shows evidence and reasoning.
8. Confidence belongs to individual findings.
9. Coverage and quality are separate.
10. Privacy and proportionality matter.
